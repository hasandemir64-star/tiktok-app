Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how YouTube-TikTok Content Automation System ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary for the app to function:

  • TikTok User ID: To identify your TikTok account
  • Username: To display your account information
  • OAuth Access Tokens: To authenticate and authorize video uploads
  • Video Metadata: Title, description, hashtags, upload timestamp
  • Publishing Analytics: Views, likes, shares, comments (from TikTok API)

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for:

  • Authenticating your TikTok account
  • Publishing videos to your TikTok account
